Welcome. You're About to Meet Yourself a Little Differently.

Imagine if your body had been quietly trying to tell you something your whole life — why you sleep the way you do, why certain foods make you feel amazing and others leave you sluggish, why stress hits you differently than it does your friends.

That's exactly what this assessment reveals.

Ayurveda is one of the world's oldest health systems, and at its heart is one beautifully simple idea: every person is born with a unique mind-body blueprint. Ayurveda calls this your Prakriti, which simply means "your nature."

There are three energies — called Doshas — that make up every human being: Vata, Pitta, and Kapha. Think of them like personality types for your body. Most of us have a mix of all three, but one or two usually show up more strongly — and that combination is uniquely yours.

This isn't a test you can pass or fail. There are no good doshas or bad doshas. Each one carries its own gifts and its own tendencies. Understanding yours simply helps you make choices — in food, sleep, movement, and daily rhythm — that feel natural and effortless rather than forced.

**A few things before you begin:**

*   Answer based on your lifelong patterns, not how you've been feeling this week or month. Think about what has been consistently true for you over the years.
*   Be honest, not aspirational. Answer for the person you are — not the person you're working towards or wish to be. There are no impressive answers here, only accurate ones.
*   If two answers feel equally true, go with your first instinct.
*   If a question doesn't feel relevant, that's fine — just choose the closest option.

The assessment has 34 questions across 7 short sections. 

Take a breath.
Answer slowly.
And enjoy getting to know yourself.
