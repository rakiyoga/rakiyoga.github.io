# Product Requirements Document — Raki Yoga

## Overview
**Raki Yoga** is a personal website for Rakesh Singh ("Raki"), an Ayurvedic health consultant based in Berlin, Germany. The site serves as a digital presence for his Ayurveda consultancy, providing educational content about Ayurveda, yoga, and holistic health practices.

**Website**: [rakiyoga.de](https://rakiyoga.de) / [rakiyoga.github.io](https://rakiyoga.github.io)

## Target Audience
- Individuals seeking holistic health solutions (Berlin-based and international)
- People interested in Ayurveda, yoga, and natural wellness
- Those dealing with autoimmune disorders, cancer, neurological/neurodegenerative conditions
- Health-conscious individuals seeking dietary guidance and dosha balancing
- German and English-speaking audiences

## Core Pages & Features

### 1. Home / Top 10 Lists (`index.html`)
- Tridoshic top-10 rankings (vegetables, fruits, carbs, weight loss)
- Visual food guides with images
- Entry point to the site

### 2. About Me (`1_aboutme.md`)
- Professional background and credentials
- Ayurveda certifications
- Areas of expertise (autoimmune disorders, cancer, neurological conditions)

### 3. Wisdom / Blog (`2_wisdom.md`)
- Blog feed with paginated Ayurveda articles
- Topics include: raw vs cooked food, kitchen utensils, Dincharya, food combinations, mushroom foraging, heating honey

### 4. Ayurveda Fundamentals (`3_ayurveda_fundamentals.md`)
- Educational content with expandable sections
- 5 Elements (Pancha Mahabhuta), Pancha Kosha, Three Doshas, Food qualities & Gunas

### 5. Recipes (`4_recipes.md`)
- Seasonal Ayurvedic recipes
- Anti-allergic soup, sprouts, wild garlic pesto, flu-bomb decoction

### 6. Consultation (`5_consultation.md`)
- Service offerings (in-person Berlin + online)
- Consultation process description
- Dosha-specific session types (Vata, Pitta, Kapha)

### 7. Contact (`6_contact.md`)
- Contact form, email, phone, social media links
- Physical address (Berlin)
- Disclaimer

## Non-Functional Requirements
- **Performance**: Static site (fast loading via GitHub Pages CDN)
- **Responsive**: Bootstrap 4 grid — mobile, tablet, desktop
- **SEO**: jekyll-sitemap plugin, proper meta tags
- **Accessibility**: Alt text on images, semantic HTML
- **Privacy**: Privacy policy page (`privacy.md`)
